Akkaris is a one-person independent game studio based in Kenitra, Morocco. I'm a software engineer by trade with a genuine interest in making games.

I build games with a focus on tight mechanics, satisfying feedback, and respect for the player's time. No filler. No padding. Just the game.

Every game starts with a single mechanic that feels good — and then gets built carefully around that feeling until it's ready to ship.
